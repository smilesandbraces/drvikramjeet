Frequently Asked Questions

Is teeth whitening safe?

When performed by a dental professional with the correct materials and protocols, whitening is generally safe. We customise the procedure to your sensitivity and desired results.

Aligners vs braces — which is better?

The choice depends on your alignment, bite and lifestyle. After examination, we’ll recommend aligners or braces based on predictability, comfort and desired timeline.

Do implants require surgery?

Implants involve a minor surgical procedure, but it’s planned carefully. Suitability depends on bone, gums and overall oral health, which we evaluate beforehand.

What to expect after an extraction?

You may experience some discomfort or swelling which subsides within a few days. We provide detailed post‑extraction instructions and options for replacement if needed.

How soon can I get dentures after extraction?

Healing time varies; immediate dentures can be provided right away while conventional dentures are fitted after the gums heal. We discuss these options during your consultation.
