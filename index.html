FAQs

Questions, answered

Common queries about appointments, costs and comfort.

Do aligners hurt?

There can be mild pressure for 1–2 days after a new set. We plan small, controlled movements to keep you comfortable.

How long will braces take?

Most cases are 12–18 months; complex cases vary. We share a written plan and timeline upfront.

Do you offer same‑day consultations?

Yes, subject to slots. Message us on WhatsApp to check availability.
